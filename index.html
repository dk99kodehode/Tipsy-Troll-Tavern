About us

Welcome to Tipsy Troll Tavern. A place where good drinks, hearty laughs, and a little mischief come together. Inspired by old-world taverns and a love for laid-back nights, were all about creating a space where everyone feels at home, whether youre here for a quick pint or a long evening with friends. At Tipsy Troll, we keep things simple. Solid drinks, friendly faces, and an atmosphere that doesnt take itself too seriously. From classic favorites to a few quirky house specials, theres always something worth trying.
